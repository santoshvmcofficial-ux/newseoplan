Website Structure
Strategy

Search engines rank individual pages, not entire websites. Each service needs its own dedicated page to capture targeted keyword traffic.

🏠 Homepage
Overview of the business, main services, and a strong first impression.
⚙️ Service Pages
Separate pages per service to rank for specific keywords like "patient care," "elder care," "home nursing."
👥 About Page
Builds trust by sharing your story, team, and company credentials.
📞 Contact Page
Easy lead capture with forms, phone, email, and Google Maps integration.
📝 Blog Section
Supports SEO by publishing helpful content that attracts organic traffic.
